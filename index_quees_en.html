What is a computer?

Most people think of a laptop or a desktop computer, but these are not the only computers that exist. A CIAA is also a computer.

What does 'Industrial' mean?

In contrast to other well-known boards such as Arduino or Raspberry Pi, CIAA is prepared for industrial use, but what exactly does this actually mean?

Why is it "Open"?

The CIAA is Open because all the design information is freely available and can be modified at will, for use in private or commercial projects.


Why is it 'Argentine'?

The CIAA Project was born in Argentina on 2013. Since then, hundreds of people, institutions, companies and the state have collaborated to make it grow.

What is the aim of this project?

The CIAA Project was started to lever the argentinean industrial environment, with the belief that academy could help to develop the industry.

Examples of CIAA uses

CIAA is applied to a diversity of use cases, from industrial systems to educational projects, domestic appliances and scientific instruments.
