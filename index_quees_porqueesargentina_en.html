Driven by institutions

ACSE and CADIEEL started and coordinate the CIAA Project. The INTI designed several models. The CONICET partially funded the project.


Developed by universities

Science and engineering professors and students from all around Argentina have developed the libraries used by the CIAA.


State-sponsored

The MinCyT (Science, Technology and Innovation Ministry), the Education Ministry and several provincial offices are supporting the CIAA project.

Industry supported

PyMES (Peque√±as y Medianas empresas - Small and Medium Size Business) have contributed prototype production, new designs and commercialization logistics.

Used across the country

The CIAA is used as the basis for equipment, products and services. Moreover, it is used for elementary, secondary and higher education.

Open to the whole world

Any person or business can make free use or make contributions to the CIAA Project from any place in the world
