The Argentine Industry

In general, argentinean small to medium enterprises do not use electronics in its production processes, products and services, diminishing its competitiviness.

How can we help?

The CIAA Project aims to change this situation, allowing enterprises to access technology in order to create new employment and to add value.

We have three goals...

To reach that aim, we proposed three goals from start, in order to orient our efforts clearly and orderly.

To drive national development

The first goal is to drive national technological development. Companies that incorporate technology will produce more value and more employment.

Provide visibility to electronics

The second goal is to add visibility to the argentinian electronics. The CIAA Project make to TV, radio, magazines and newspapers, TED talks, prizes, etc.

Generate structural changes

The third goal is to make structural changes in the way knowledge is created, shared and applied in our country.
