Open Courses - CAPSE

The Open Courses on Embedded Systems Programming held twice a year last four months, are e-learning mode, and any interested can register to them.

School of Embedded Systems

The School of Embedded Systems Teaching is oriented mainly to teachers and is held twice a year in different Argentine cities.

Argentinian Symposium (SASE)

The Argentine Symposium on Embedded Systems is held every year at Buenos Aires, Argentina. CIAA talks and programming courses are offered.

INET - Education Ministry

The National Institute of Technological Education (INET) organizes courses to train teachers in the use of the CIAA in technical schools throughout Argentina.

Specialization and Masters

The Faculty of Engineering of the University of Buenos Aires (FIUBA) offers classroom- and e-learning postgraduate courses using the CIAA.

Postgraduate and Training

The Faculty of Exact Sciences, Engineering and Surveying of the National University of Rosario offers training courses and Systematic Trails.
