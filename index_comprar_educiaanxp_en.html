  • EDU-CIAA-NXP Model

32-bit dual-core processor

The same processor as the CIAA-NXP, an ARM Cortex-M4F/Cortex-M0 32-bit dual core with 1MB flash and 136kB SRAM, and a JTAG debug interface.

Communication interfaces

It has several communication ports such as USB, RS485, CAN, SPI, I2C and support for Ethernet based conectivity, LCD display and RS232.

Switches and LEDs included

It includes four push buttons, several LEDs, ADC and DAC, many digital inputs and outputs, and is powered via USB.

Availability of 'Ponchos'

"Ponchos" are modules that can be connected over CIAA boards. They are similar to an "Arduino shield" or an expansion board.
