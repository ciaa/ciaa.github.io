Contact the organizers

This mailbox allows you to contact the CIAA Project coordinators in order to ask for interviews, press releases or any specific non-technical matters.

Is everything resolved?

No, you may occasionally stumble upon a problem that no one saw or fixed before. This is common in open as well as in closed projects.

Who can help me?

Sometimes the authors of the CIAA Project can help you fix the problem, but sometimes they do not have the time or have another priority.

What if no one can help me?

If you know to fix the problem, ask for help to do it youself and then share the solution. If you don't know how to fix it, try to find another way.
