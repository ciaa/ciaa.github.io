Surely, we think of this...

When we hear the word "computer", surely, the first thing we think about is a laptop or desktop computer.

But there is a computer here

But inside a washing machine there is a computer too. You can choose different washing programs that its computer will execute.

And lots of computers here ...

And within a car there are many computers that control the engine, radio, brakes, windows, air conditioner, etc.

A computer is...

Simply put, a computer is an electronic system that executes a program, and interacts with the outside world through a variety of inputs and outputs.

CIAAs are computers

Thus, CIAAs are computers, because they have a processor and memory, they can execute programs, and they have a variety of inputs and outputs.

How do I program a CIAA?

If you would like to learn how to program a CIAA, we can help by providing guidance, even if you have no previous experience.
