  • CIAA-NXP Model

32-bit dual-core processor

It has an ARM Cortex-M4F/M0 @204MHz processor with a 1 MB Flash memory, 136 KB SRAM, JTAG debug, EEPROM, SDRAM, and it can run Embedded Linux.

Communication Interfaces

It has several communication ports, including USB, Ethernet, RS485, RS232, CAN, SPI, I2C, and support for connection of LCD display and GPIO input/output.

CIAA-NXP diagram

It includes digital and analog inputs and outputs (optoisolated, relays, open drain, 4-20 mA, 0-10 V, etc.), A-D and D-A conversors, supplied over USB or 24 VDC.

Designed for industrial Use

The CIAA-NXP was designed to be used for industrial applications, and includes high quality terminals, beside of several protections and safety considerations.
