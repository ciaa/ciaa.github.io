CIAA-NXP Model

This board is the first member of the CIAA family. It's designed for industrial use, what implies the inclusion of several safety features.

EDU-CIAA-NXP Model

This is the second member of the CIAA family. It was created specifically to be used in education, as an introductory board for the CIAA-NXP.

picoCIAA Model

Aimed at small footprint projects, and also to drive inputs and outputs in general purpose computers equipped with PCI Express interface.

Other CIAA Models

There are several other models in the CIAA family, whose designs are optimized for specific applications or are based on different processors.

Ponchos' for the CIAA

We call "Ponchos" to the modules designed to be connected over the CIAA boards. The name "Poncho" is a synonym for "Shield" or expansion board.
